This year, I want to incorporate more finance and economics articles into my reading itinerary to expand my reach and grasp new subjects. Skiing

Skiing is my favorite activity/hobby, and I try to ski as much as possible. Skiing, along with being fun, has taught me many invaluable lessons. Skiing has taught me discipline: Waking up extremely early, practicing over and over again, sticking to something and never quitting, as well as enduring sub-zero temperatures in thin racing gear. For me, Skiing is a skill that I have been building for well over a decade, and when I look back, I can see how far I have come. Other than invaluable life lessons, the sport is and always has been a key part of my identity; I wouldn't be complete without it. Unfortunately, I am unable to race this year because of other obligations, but I always try to find time to practice and make myself a little better.
