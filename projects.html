Academic Projects

Totalitarianism Essay

This research project examines the rise of totalitarian regimes by analyzing how fear, isolation, and propaganda are used to control populations. It focuses particularly on the Khmer Rouge in Cambodia, exploring how the government reshaped society through extreme political, social, and cultural manipulation. The essay evaluates the consequences of totalitarian rule and the long-term impact on the affected populations. This essay was one of my favorite pieces to write, so I hope that you will enjoy it

Island School Project

This lab report aims to determine the differences in fish biodiversity between two coral reefs in the Bahamas. I wrote it with 3 peers in the spring of 2025. It is my most extensive research paper and definitely the coolest project that I have ever done.
