Totalitarianism Essay

This research project examines the rise of totalitarian regimes by analyzing how fear, isolation, and propaganda are used to control populations. It focuses particularly on the Khmer Rouge in Cambodia, exploring how the government reshaped society through extreme political, social, and cultural manipulation. The essay evaluates the consequences of totalitarian rule and the long-term impact on the affected populations.
