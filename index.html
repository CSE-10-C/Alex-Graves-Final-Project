About Me

Although Palantir has been my most lucrative purchase, others have yielded high returns, such as the SPDR Gold Trust and AFFRM. Outside of investing, I am a ravenous reader. Last year, I read 104 full-length books, spanning a range of genres, including fantasy, the classics, psychology, and political science.
